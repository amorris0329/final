ABOUT US

Eleanor “Nora” Page has a dream - to make pre-modern texts accessible in the modern world. A former Professor of Latin and Medieval Literature at Columbia University, her mission is to make it easier for students, scholars, and enthusiasts to get into books of all genres, from the philosophical texts of the ancient Greeks to the comedies of Greats like Shakespeare and Cervantes. Thanks to the time, effort, and care of her and our whole Elder Pages family, our store has become the most prestigious place to find historic books around!

We have a wide selection of books from famous classical, medieval, and early modern authors.
Come for our Book Club every Wednesday night, where we go over famous books form across the
ages! Right now, we’re reading through Dante’s Inferno, the first book of the Divine Comedy!

If you’d like to learn about history, literature, and culture from ages past, check out
Dr. Page’s YouTube Channel at: www.youtube.com/@NoraPagePHD
